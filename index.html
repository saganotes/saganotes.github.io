About Saga
Saga is a notes app focused on organizing and planning. Create notebooks, called sagas, that represent something you want to be a hero at.

Highlights

Quick Convert
Use the convert button to convert between headings, list items and text. This allows you to create structured content fast and efficiently.
Drag & Drop
Move list and checklist items within a note via drag & drop to organize content and change priorities of tasks.

Swipe to Remove

About Saga
Saga Notes is the app about you. It makes it easy to capture your thoughts, keep a journal and plan your life.

Features

Sagas
Create sagas to structure your life. All your sagas will be in a drawer on the left. Swipe anywhere from left to right to access it.
Notes
Add as many notes to your sagas as you want. Sort them alphabetically or by date.
Notes

Quick Convert
Use the convert button to easily convert between text, headings, list and todo items.
Drag & Drop
Move list items, todos and links via drag & drop to organize and change priorities.
